## FAQs
**What is Pashu Parichay?**
Pashu Parichay is a smart QR ID system that creates a digital identity for your pet, enabling quick owner contact, health records, and lost pet recovery.​

**How does the QR tag work?**
Scan the unique QR on your pet's collar with any smartphone camera to view safe public details and contact the owner—no app required.​

**Do I need an app?**
No, it works directly in any mobile browser for scanning and basic access.​

**How do I register my pet?**
Scan the QR, create a free account, and add details like name, breed, vaccines, and health info to activate the profile.​

**What info is public when scanned?**
Only limited safe details like pet name, photo, and behavior are shown, with secure contact options.​

**How does it help if my pet is lost?**
Scanners see basic info and can notify you instantly or send their location.​

**Is it free?**
Yes, basic features are free; premium adds unlimited pets, reminders, and AI advice.
